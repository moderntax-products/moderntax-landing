What SBA 7(a) Lending Requires

The SBA Standard Operating Procedure (SOP 50 10) mandates IRS transcript verification to confirm that borrower-provided tax returns match what was actually filed with the IRS.

Sole Proprietors

Schedule C filers verified through 1040 transcripts including all business income schedules.

Partnerships (1065)

Partnership return transcripts plus K-1 verification for each guarantor partner.

C-Corps (1120)

Corporate return transcripts for the operating company and any affiliated entities.

S-Corps (1120S)

S-corp return transcripts with shareholder distribution and compensation data.
