What Employment Verification Requires

HR platforms, background check companies, and lenders need reliable income and employment data. IRS records are the gold standard.

W-2 Income Verification

Confirm employer name, wages, and withholdings directly from IRS Wage & Income transcripts. Covers current and prior-year W-2s.

1099 Contractor Income

Verify 1099-NEC, 1099-MISC, and 1099-K income for self-employed individuals and gig workers. See total non-employee compensation by payer.

Multi-Year History

Access up to 10 years of wage and income records to build a complete employment and income history for any individual.

How ModernTax Helps

API-First Design

RESTful API with webhook notifications. Submit verification requests programmatically and receive structured JSON results.

Employer Detection

Automatically identify employers from W-2 data, including EIN matching and employer name normalization across tax years.

PEO Flagging

Detect when a Professional Employer Organization (PEO) is the W-2 employer of record, so you know the true hiring entity.

Structured Data

Parsed, structured JSON responses with employer details, income amounts, and filing metadata — no PDF scraping required.

More Reliable Than VOE

IRS records cannot be fabricated. Eliminate the risk of fraudulent pay stubs or unresponsive employer HR departments.

Webhook Notifications

Get notified the moment results are ready. No polling required — integrate directly into your existing workflow automation.
